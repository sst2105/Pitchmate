Why not just use ChatGPT?

Fair question.
Here's the honest answer.

You could open ChatGPT, paste your resume, paste the JD, ask it to research the company, ask it to find gaps, ask it to write a pitch — and spend 45 minutes doing it across five tabs, burning your free limit before you're done.

🎯

Built for exactly this, nothing else

General AI gives general answers. Pitchmate is designed around one specific workflow — startup job applications. Every prompt, every output, every agent is tuned for that context.

🧠

Session memory across the whole pipeline

Upload your resume once. It flows through every agent automatically. No re-pasting, no re-explaining, no context lost between steps. ChatGPT forgets everything when you open a new chat.

🔍

Real-time company research, not training data

ChatGPT's knowledge has a cutoff. Pitchmate searches the web right now — funding rounds from last month, Glassdoor reviews from last week, hiring signals from today.

🆓

Completely free. No premium tier for the good stuff.

No "upgrade to unlock company research." No token limits that cut you off mid-analysis. Everything Pitchmate does is free — because students applying to startups shouldn't have to pay to get honest career advice.
